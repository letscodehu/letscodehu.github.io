# Prospectus document structure

**Change:** [prospectus.md](../prospectus.md) was rewritten as valid Markdown with a workshop-first layout.

**Structure:**

- Short trainer and delivery-method sections up front.
- Two standalone workshop offerings: **Architect Mindset** (2 days) and **AI Engineering** (2 days), each with who / learn / format / outcomes.
- **Build Your Own Workshop — Topic Menu** at the end: grouped topic bullets (no durations) so clients can mix modules from either track.
- The former standalone "Decision Making & ADR (1 day)" offering is represented only as topics in the menu (composable with other topics).
- Intro was further refactored into four explicit sections: **Introduction**, **About Krisztian Papp**, **Software Architecture and AI on YouTube**, and **Training and Courses**, while keeping workshop sections as the primary content body.
- Narration was normalized to a consistent third-person voice (`Krisztian` / `he`) across intro, about, training, module descriptions, and CTA lines.
